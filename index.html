My Information

I graduated from the University of Oregon in 2019 with a B.A. in History, with minors in Latin, Medieval Studies, and Creative Writing. Early on in the COVID-19 pandemic I started pursuing my MLIS degree, which I obtained in March, 2023 from the University of Denver (DU). In my time at DU, I became involved in two student organizations, the American Library Association's Student Chapter, and the Library and Information Sciences Student & Alumni Association, serving as communications and outreach officer for both. Additionally, I maintained a 4.0GPA throughout the program, and, consequently, was both nominated into the National Society of Leadership and Success, and awarded the Outstanding LIS Online Program Student Award. As part of my MLIS degree, I completed a ten week internship with the Roseburg Public Library, participating in activities all across the spectrum of librarianship. Following graduation, my goal is to acquire work in a public library setting, in archival studies, or in the publishing field.
