My Information

I have recently become a ghostwriter, editor, and copywriter after years of interest and consideration. Writing is my life, and helping people learn is my passion. I take pride in my reliability and consistency, both of which have always ensured I meet every deadline without a sacrifice to quality. Due to my novelty as a freelance writer, my writing samples are of an educational and creative writing nature. I graduated from the University of Oregon in 2019 with a B.A. in history, and minors in Latin, medieval studies, and creative writing. In 2023 I obtained my Master's in Library and Information Sciences (MLIS) degre from the University of Denver (DU). In my time at DU, I became involved in two student organizations, the American Library Association's Student Chapter and the Library and Information Sciences Student & Alumni Association, serving as communications and outreach officer for both. Additionally, I maintained a 4.00 GPA as a graduate student, was inducted into the National Society of Leadership and Success, was awarded the Outstanding LIS Online Program Student Award, and completed a ten-week internship with the Roseburg Public Library.
